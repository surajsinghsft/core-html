Web Development

Web development is the process of building and maintaining websites. It involves both front-end (what users see) and back-end (server-side) development. Popular languages for web development include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and various back-end technologies like Node.js, Python, and PHP.

As a web developer, it's important to stay updated with the latest frameworks, tools, and best practices to deliver high-quality websites and applications.
